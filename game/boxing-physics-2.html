Gameplay Overview

In Boxing Physics 2, players take control of comically animated boxers and engage in fast-paced and unpredictable matches against each other. With intentionally wonky physics mechanics, every punch and movement is exaggerated and absurd, leading to chaotic and hilarious battles that are as entertaining as they are unpredictable.

Compete against a friend in intense one-on-one matches or test your skills against the computer-controlled opponent in single-player mode. With simple controls and outrageous gameplay, Boxing Physics 2 is easy to pick up and play, making it perfect for players of all ages and skill levels.
